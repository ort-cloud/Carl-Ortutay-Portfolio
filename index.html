Some Gnarly Projects

Fitness Pizza

No matter your education level Fitness Pizza aims to join two former adversaries, pizza and fitness, together to help you slice your way into better health. React and CSS take care of the front of the house, while Node and PostgreSQL hold down the back. All a user need do is tell Fitness Pizza how hard, or soft, they trained that day, and it bakes all that data into a tangible, understandable, result: Slices of delicious pizza!

Star Wars Quiz App

A simple web app testing the user's knowledge of the first three Star Wars Films. The app uses a combination of jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. The user is taken through a series of 10 questions. With each selection the user is then given feedback informing them if they got the answer correct, if not they are given the correct answer. The user's score is calculated throughout and will lead them to one of three responses depending upon the accumulated score.

The ISS Scout

Using jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS this minimalist, one-page, app invites you to explore the final frontier. Using calls to outside APIs the app allows visitors to enter their address, or another point of interest, and receive a list of times for potential sightings of the International Space Station. Whether you're planning a date or spending time with friends and family, our application's mission is to bring about wonder and excitement.
